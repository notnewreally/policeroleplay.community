Fun Border Roleplay - Anuhai Investigation

Fun Border Roleplay (FBRP), owned by Anuhai and DezJerban, has been the subject of an extensive investigation by the Police Roleplay Community (PRC). Our findings have uncovered alarming violations, including but not limited to:

Confirmed Violations

  • Racism
  • Abuse
  • Child Labour
  • Sexism

Actions Taken

  • FBRP ER:LC server has been permanently shut down.
  • A formal request has been submitted to Discord to delete the server.
  • Associated entities of FBRP have ceased under PRC jurisdiction.

PRC stands firm against any form of discrimination, exploitation, or abuse. Our commitment is to uphold a safe, inclusive, and fair gaming environment for all.
